About

Xh30 / Cheo / José Escobar

Venezuelan producer born in 2000, focused on videogame music and experimental electronic music. Lover of video games, anime, and music: expressed in a variety of different genres from around the world. My taste in music is really crazy, like, I listen to EDM, Rock, Jazz, Bossa nova, movie soundtracks, game soundtracks, anime soundtracks, what else?

What do I use to make music?

I use the DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) created by Image-Line "FL Studio 20". You can visit their website here!

List of Plugins I use:

  • Harmless
  • NI Massive
  • DSK AkoustiK Guitar
  • magical8bitPlug
  • Medusa2
  • refx Nexus
  • Synth1
  • Sytrus

Most of them are free :)
